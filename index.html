Take A Trip Over The Rainbow To Oz

Take a Stroll Through Muchkinland!

Should you get caught up by a tornado in your state, and find your house, mansion, or apartment above a witch...you might want to come out an give the Munchkins a quick hello! This beautiful town is filled with vibrant, colorful, and cheerful atmosphere, featuring lush green fields and blue skies, rolling landscapes, and is the starting point for Dorothy's journey on the Yellow Brick Road to the Emerald City. It is the "corn belt" of Oz, full of farms, small towns, and lakes.



March Up to The Winkie Castle

You can pass the flying monkeys and explore this now abandoned exhibit of The Winkie Castle. It is the formidable, ominous fortress that serves as the Wicked Witch of the West's lair. The castle is situated on the peak of a large mountain (Knobblehead Pike) in the Vinkus, the western quadrant of the Land of Oz. This region is known as the Winkie Country. There might still be a bucket of water knocked down there...the last remnants of who that castle once belonged to some time ago...



Spend Your Short Day at the Emerald City

The Emerald City is the capital of Oz, characterized by a facade of green and gold splendor built by the Wizard, with buildings paved with emeralds. The city is named for its appearance, with buildings made of green marble and gold, and streets paved with glittering emeralds. However, under this dazzling surface, the city has a darker, more dangerous side, with poverty and crime-infested areas existing alongside the prosperous elite. With the Wizard now gone, (along with the tyrrany) It remains a monument of this dazzling city and world!
